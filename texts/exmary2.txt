Then she laide herself upon the blocke most quietlie, and 
strectching out her armes and legges cryed out In manus tuas 
domine three or foure times, and at the laste while one of the 
executioners held her slightlie with one of his handes, the 
other gave two strokes with an axe before he cutt off her 
head, and yet lefte a little gristle behinde at which time she 
made verie small noyse and stirred not any parte of herself 
from the place where she laye.... Her lipps stirred up and 
downe almost a quarter of an hour after her head was cut off.
